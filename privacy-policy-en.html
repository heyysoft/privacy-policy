Privacy Policy

This page explains what data the Namaz Rehberim application may access, how that data is used, and what controls users have over these permissions and processing activities.

Effective Date: 23.03.2026 Last Updated: 23.03.2026

1. Information We Access

Our application may access the following information:

  • Device location: Used to calculate the Qibla direction and display prayer times based on your current location.
  • Compass/direction data: Used to determine the direction of the device and show the Qibla orientation.
  • Approximate place information: City/country style place names may be derived from your location data and displayed inside the app.
  • Device language/region settings: May be used to determine the application language.

2. How We Use Information

Any information accessed by the application is used only for the following purposes:

  • To calculate and display the Qibla direction
  • To calculate prayer times based on your current location
  • To display city/country style place information related to your location
  • To adapt the application language to your device settings
  • To provide the core functionality of the application

3. How Data Is Processed

Qibla direction and prayer time calculations are performed primarily on the device. The current version of the application does not include user account creation, manual transmission of data to a backend server, advertising, or behavioral analytics tracking.

However, some operating system level location services, such as deriving city/country names from location coordinates, may be processed by your device platform provider. In such cases, Apple, Google, or the device manufacturer's own privacy terms may apply.

4. Location Permission

The application accesses your device location only if you grant permission. If location permission is not granted, certain features of the application, especially Qibla direction and location-based prayer times, may not function properly.

You may revoke location permission at any time from your device settings.

5. Compass and Sensor Data

The application uses compass and related sensor data only to display the Qibla direction. This data is not used to build user profiles or for advertising purposes.

6. Data Sharing

The application does not collect, sell, or commercially share your personal data on Heyy Software servers.

The following situations are exceptions:

  • When required to comply with legal obligations
  • When technical processing occurs through device operating system or platform-level location services
  • When processing occurs within app store or platform provider infrastructure

7. Data Retention

As of this version, the application does not store users' personal data on a central server. Location and sensor data may be used temporarily while the application features are running.

8. Children's Privacy

The application is intended for general audiences. The developer does not knowingly collect separate account information or identity data from children.

9. Security

Reasonable technical and administrative measures are taken to help protect user data. However, no method of data processing over the internet, mobile devices, or platform services can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Third-Party Services

The application may rely on services provided by the device operating system and the platform on which the application runs. These may include location services, sensor services, and app store infrastructure. The data processing activities of such services are subject to the privacy policies of the relevant third parties.

11. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the following rights:

  • To learn what data is being processed
  • To withdraw permissions you previously granted
  • To stop using the application
  • To contact us and request information

You may manage location and sensor permissions directly from your device settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version may be shared within the application or through the relevant distribution channel. Continued use of the application after any update means you accept the revised policy.
